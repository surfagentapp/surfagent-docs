# License & Activation

## Free Trial

SurfAgent includes a **7-day free trial** — no credit card, no account required.

The trial starts automatically on first launch, keyed to your machine. A countdown badge appears in the app sidebar showing days remaining. When the trial expires, a prompt will guide you to purchase.

## Purchasing

**Price:** $49 one-time (no subscription)

**What you get:**
- Lifetime license for 1 machine
- All future updates included
- MCP server (`surfagent-mcp`) — free and open source forever

**Buy from the app:** Click the purchase button in the trial expiry modal or sidebar.

**Buy from the web:** [app.surfagent.app/portal](https://app.surfagent.app/portal)

Payments accepted via crypto (Bitcoin, USDC, ETH, SOL and more via NowPayments).

## Activating Your License

After purchase, your license key will be shown in the portal and emailed to you.

1. Open SurfAgent
2. Click **Settings → License**
3. Enter your key and click **Activate**
4. The app validates the key against the license server and binds it to your machine ID

## Promo / Referral Codes

If you have a promo or referral code, enter it at checkout before paying. The discount will be applied automatically before the payment invoice is generated.

## Moving to a New Machine

Each license supports 1 active machine. To transfer:

1. Click **Settings → License → Deactivate** on your old machine
2. Activate on the new machine with the same key

If your old machine is gone, contact [support@surfagent.app](mailto:support@surfagent.app) with your purchase proof to reset the activation.
